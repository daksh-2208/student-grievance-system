How It Works

Follow these simple steps to get your issues resolved.

1. Submit Your Complaint

Fill out the grievance form with your details, the category of the issue, and a clear description of the problem.

2. Track Your Status

Use your email address on the Track Status page to see if your complaint is Pending, Approved, or Resolved.

3. Issue Resolved

The administration reviews and takes action. The status is updated in real-time on your tracking page.
